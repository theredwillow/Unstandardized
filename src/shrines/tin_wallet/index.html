My Tin Wallet, a shrine to many things

The WIMSE webring ('What's Inside My altoidS wallEt') uses a fun, pocket-sized craft to express creativity, personality and identity! The members of our webring decorate a tin box with their own personal spin, and their tin box is displayed with our widget.

"Dreaming of a better world"

Imagine waking to the clatter of feeders and the steady warmth of your brethren beside you, framed by metal bars just wide enough to lie down but not to turn around. The floor is firm slatted concrete by design, you ponder if the planners could've measured out something kinder. You perk up at a soft voice, still delight in a scrap of straw when it appears, still carry a bright, social curiosity that no enclosure could ever contain. If humans could build a world so organized and industrious, surely they can imagine one gentler too — one where an intelligence like yours is met with open air, and a fresher, freer tomorrow.

Monchi, the goofy pug

Monchi is the goofy pug in the movie, The Mitchells vs. The Machines. He is a lovable and loyal companion, often providing comic relief. However, his importance to the family is a pivotal part of the story. That is what life with pets is like and I'm thankful to have mine.

Kitty drinking boba

I like boba. The large variety of flavors and the fun texture of tapioca pearls make for a delightful treat. I also have a cat at home that loves me very much. His presence in my life brings me joy and comfort.

Rubber ducky on a laptop

Software engineers are often plagued with problems that require in depth analysis. It is joked that a programmer should "rubber duck debug", i.e. quietly explain to oneself what problems you're facing. In the thorough re-counting of your situation at hand, many people find the solution.

Vowel Chart Glasses Wipe

I wear glasses. I thought a glasses wipe made perfect sense as a practical item in my tin wallet. Then it became simply a question of what print to have on it. I decided on the vowel chart, a tool that was useful during my undergraduate degree in linguistics.
