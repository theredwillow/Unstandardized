Welcome to my Unstandardized Website!

In 2007, the website Facebook rose in popularity. I hated this. All of the customization that I achieved on my MySpace was now ineffectual because this company churned out template garbage. Everyone's profiles looked the same. There was no point in visiting them anymore. So we stopped visiting carefully curated spaces online.

The skills that I learned while the internet still had personality now serve me professionally. But web standards are a double-edged sword, i.e. they are extremely boring.

I rediscovered the world of Neocities after a certain youtube video discussed the "intentional internet". A hypothetical realm where people still sat at their computers and let their creative juices flow.

This web page is my first attempt at re-entering the world of craft, artistry, and giving a damn. I don't know what this page will be yet. I imagine a brain dump, like other Neocities pages. But perhaps there will be a certain emphasis in appreciating others' spaces, a highlight of what I like about other people's pages.

I'm still getting a hold on Neocities culture, so bare with me. I've recognized that web buttons are all the rage. I'm personally a big fan of 88x31's, so I'm gonna start there.
